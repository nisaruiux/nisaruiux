Hey there! My name is Muhammed nisar . I'm a UI UX Designer with over 1 year of wonderful experience in the field. I am passionate about designing beautiful, intuitive interfaces that help people be more productive. As a UI UX designer, I am always striving to create interfaces that are easy to use and look beautiful. I take into account the userâ€™s needs and wants when designing my interfaces, and I always aim to create a positive user experience. My approach to design is user-centric, and I always put the user first. I strive to create intuitive and easy-to-use interfaces that help users achieve their goals. I'll be happy to work with you on your idea. I started my story with graphic designing to get into this field and learn the tools. I understand that this story has no end because learning can continue to learn and it is interesting.

I am also passionate about making my designs responsive and adaptive to different devices and screen sizes. Ui and ux are co-related. I always strive to create a user-friendly experience that is easy to use and looks great on any device. My approach to design is user-centric and I put the user first, my process includes wireframing your idea, prototyping a model with a lot of thought invested into user experience, User research and user strategy, Wireframing, User testing, Prototyping, UI design, Responsive web design, Front-end programming (Coding) Information architecture. I believe that design should evolve from just being a colorful interface to an unforgettable experience for the end-user.

I primarily use Figma and Adobe XD, along with a palette of other tools that enhance my productivity. As a designer i encompasses all aspects of the end-user's interaction with the company, its services, and its products. Art gives me more satisfaction. That one feeling makes me laugh more creatively.
